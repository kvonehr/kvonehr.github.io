LEAD ACID BATTERY -> SOLAR CELL

The idea for this project was inspired by an article posted on the IEEE Spectrum site and from the final lab of my Electronic Materials course, where solar cells were fabricated using Rasberry Juice Dye, TiO_2, and a Potassium Iodide and Iodine solution. A small DC motor ran when connected to the terminals and the device was placed under a high intensity lamp, this still amazes me.

I follow the "Green Tech" subtopic posts on the IEEE Spectrum site as frequently as possibly, and am in the process of starting a new project with the GVSU Alternative Energy Club to build some solar cells from scratch. The ultimate goal being to build solar cells with common, non-toxic household items that can be easily obtained; which may seem outlandish at this point.
