Sustainability through Engineering

An Effort to Exceed the Dilettante

To be recruited for employment on credentials alone is often indicative of an organization structure that undervalues the attitudes and beliefs of its employees. To see the employee as simply a position to be filled, results in a workplace full of turnover and waste. Robust relationships between businesses are chipped away and what remains is a workforce that lacks pride in it's work. If you, the employer, have taken the time to read this statement, you likely place value upon fostering a workplace of like-minded individuals, concerned with the social and environmental impact of everyday interactions and decisions. It is assumed that there is considerable interest in the benefits I might provide your organization and it is hoped that the site provides the information necessary to assess integration with your future.

Why?

The intention of this site is to present additional insight of my knowledge and personallity. The motivation was a reaction to classical résumés, which inadequately represent any modern computer scientist or engineer with considerable interest in his/her field. The lightweight design of the site allows a user to quickly scroll and glance over information of interest, simultaneously allowing for a passionate and demonstrative presentation of self.
