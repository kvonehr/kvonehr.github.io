CAREER + RESEARCH

Passion and Funding Collide

Ambient Energy and Low Power

During the previous year (August 2013 - 2014), a large amount my of time has been spent investigating the limitations of our current technology. What is possible with low power energy sources using commercially available devices, such as the MSP430. This, in addition to exploring various sources of alternative energy - vibration, solar, chemical, heat, etc.

The organization for which this work was being done, took an interest in the potential of Piezoelectric Vibration Energy Harvesting. This required me to perform analysis of several types of mechanical systems, design a piezo-electric generator using these results, and then perform a useful function with the gathered energy. The intellectual property of these findings is not owned strictly by me, and therefore I am not at liberty to discuss the details.

However, this does not mean that my interest ends there, I wish to continue with the application of low power systems in interesting and unique ways, several of which will be discussed on this site, mainly the NFC Door Lock. I wish to make the door lock a self-sustaining system, the method of which will be investigated and published through this site.
