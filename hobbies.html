PERSONAL HOBBIES

LEARNING FOR A LIFETIME

Music

Classically trained since the age of 10, music has alwasy been very important to me. I am trained on the Alto Sax and Oboe, but have also taught myself guitar and piano, but don't consider myself proficient in a professional setting. It works well in a studio setting for developing and working through melodiesand new rhythms. I had wished to play music in college, but did not have the time to be a part of an organized group, with engineering and swimming taking up much of my time. Instead, I choose to produce electronic music in my spare time as one of my creative outlets. I use Ableton Live as my Digital Audio Workstation. This has been a very helpful activity in learning Digital Signal Processing, and I find that a lot of the concepts that are essential in understanding DSP, I have subconsiously considered about for a long time, simply because of the prolific use of filtering and processing inherent within electronic music.

Below is a track that I most recently worked on. It stems from a love of the "Deep House" genre, which I find to be extremely helpful in maintaining focus when working through long tedious tasks. This is primarily why I choose the moniker "Daemon". It represents the idea of what I try to do with my music - or rather what I love in music - a hip soundtrack to my own life. My all time favorite artist being Bonobo, I find that his simple, yet meticulously created rhythms and melodies, solidly grounded in a genre between jazz and deep house, speak most clearly to my everyday emotions.
