kvonehr.github.io
=================

###A resume extension and personal webpage.

The intention of this site is to present additional insight of my knowledge and personallity. The motivation was a reaction to classical résumés, which inadequately represent any modern computer scientist or engineer with considerable interest in his/her field.

The lightweight design of the site allows a user to quickly scroll and glance over information of interest, simultaneously allowing for a passionate and demonstrative presentation of self.
